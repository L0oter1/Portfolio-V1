“Go to bed smarter than when you woke up.” — Charlie Munger

When I watched a documentary about Warren Buffet in middle school during a scout meeting, he said a quote similar to this which resonated with me. When I was a middle schooler I didn't really read other than the required one but, when I got to college, I thought I could spend more time reading instead of watching social media shorts or entertaining videos. So I try to read at least a couple pages a day.

I don't view every book as learning something but I feel reading books can sometimes offer a new perspective on things or make you ask yourself interesting questions based on how and what the author writes.
